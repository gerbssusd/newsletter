Releasing Hapara

The old "I can't work on my computer becasue my alchemy teacher has it locked in a Hapara session" exuse is no longer valid! You can release stusdents from other teacher Hapara sessions by clicking the "Release All" button in "Browser Tabs." You can also release individual students by clicking the cinnamon bun symbol on their individual tabs listing.
